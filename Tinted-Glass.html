Advantages Of Tinted and Clear Glass

Tinted glass is a type of glass that has been treated with a colored film or coating to reduce glare and control light transmission. This modification can enhance privacy and aesthetics while offering practical benefits
Clear glass is a type of glass that is uncolored and free of tints or coatings, providing unobstructed transparency and clarity. It allows maximum light transmission and visibility.
