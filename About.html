About Our company

SKV Glass & Interiors

The history of the company goes back into the year 2021, when SKTL came into being and it was primarily engaged in Furniture & Interior projects. Later, we expanded our horizons and got into the manufacturing of Decorative glass in the year 2024 & a new company was formed with the name of SKV Glass & interiors. We have a manufacturing unit, near IMT Manesar, Gurugram, equipped with all advanced machineries & equipments to produce high quality decorative glass. SKV glass is run by a team of professionals having industry experience of more than 15 years & deep insight about the products.

Premium Design

Our skilled & dedicated workforce of about 30 persons brings our imagination into a real masterpiece. We have in-house machineries comprising of Grinding machine, lamination, Paint Booth, Glass washing, Corner edge machine. Paint lab, Cutting, Milling, Punching, Drilling to manufacture, monitor and produce each & every product in a quality controlled environment. Our Product range encompasses Fabric Laminated Glass, Tinted Glass, Wire Mesh Glass, Clear Glass, Marble Glass, Reeded Glass & Laquered Glass. Today SKV Glass is the first choice of every interior designer and architect alike for their aesthetic value, versatility and longer life.

Premium Glass & Mirror Solutions
