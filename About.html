About us SKV Glass is led by a team of seasoned professionals with over 15 years of industry experience, supported by a skilled and dedicated workforce of 30+ craftsmen who transform creative concepts into refined masterpieces.

Our diverse product portfolio includes Sliding Doors, Synchronized Doors, Glass Doors, Shower Cubicles, Decorative Mirrors, Bathroom Vanity Mirrors, Kitchen Cabinets, Wardrobes, Mini Bar Shutters, and Workstation Screens. These are crafted using Fabric Laminated Glass, Tinted Glass, Wire Mesh Glass, Clear Glass, Marble Glass, Reeded Glass, and Lacquered Glass.

Vision To become a globally recognized brand in premium and niche decorative glass solutions, expanding capacities and capabilities to serve the high-end luxury interior segment.
