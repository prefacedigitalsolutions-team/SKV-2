About Us

SKV Glass & Interiors was established in 2024 as a strategic expansion of SKTL, a company founded in 2021 with a strong foundation in Furniture & Interior Projects. With a passion for design excellence and innovation, SKV Glass & Interiors was formed to specialize in the manufacturing of high-quality decorative glass products, catering to modern and luxury interior requirements.

We operate a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility near IMT Manesar, Gurugram, equipped with advanced machinery and cutting-edge technology to ensure precision, consistency, and superior finish across all products. Every stage of production is carried out in-house within a strictly quality-controlled environment.
